Mastser Mobile UX

Mobile first mentality. Need to rewire your brain to not think of desktop content and sizes first. Mobile first forces a content first design to make sure content will be visible on all devices

Focus groups unreliable. Watch what they do not what they say. Top area of phones and tablets are hard to reach.

Better results when main controls are at the bottom easy to reach areas. Software typically isn't made well, we just settle for it. It's up to design and programming to rise above it.

Engineer/design stardards aren't always known to consumers. Icons like the hamburger menu aren't always understood

Mobile constraints force simplification

Reduce cognitive load. Remove unnecessary things, especially input fields in mobile.

Increase the size of buttons and fields in mobile to make touch input easier

Use the right keyboard type(numberpad for credit cards and currency. Datepicker for dates)
